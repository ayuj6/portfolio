About Me

I am an international student from India currently in my Senior year, majoring in Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin, Madison. I did my early years of school in India, where I gained interest in Computer Science through courses offered to me. I then decided I wanted to pursue this further and applied abroad to major in this field. It was only in my Junior year where I decided to take a few courses that aligned with my hobbies and other interests and realized that I could declare for a certificate where I take similar courses - this was when I decided to pursue a Digital Studies Certificate.


Through my experiences at various companies, I have gained a keen interest in AI, ML, and Data Analtyics. I hope to be able to pursue these areas after graduation. However, with my increasing interest in Digital Studies, I hope to also be able to pursue something (as a hobby or professionally) in this area. Ideally, I would like to pursue something along the lines of UI/UX design - which would make use of my learnings from my major and certificate.
